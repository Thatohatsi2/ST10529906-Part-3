About us

This business is for people with mental health problems

Mission

To support South African young people are finished with their grade 12/ matric by providing compassionate mental health care, alternative career pathway planning, and meaningful community connection-empowering young people to build fulfilling lives and contribute positively to their local communities.

Vision

A South Africa where every young person has access to support, opportunities, and validation they need to thrive, regardless of whether they pursue traditional tertiary education.

Core Values

  • Inclusivity: Ensuring all young people regardless of background, location, or circumstances, can access support.
  • Validation: Celebrating diverse paths to success and recognising the value of all contributions to community development.
  • Community Leadership: Prioritising local knowledge and empowering community members to drive change in their own areas
  • Compassion: Centering mental health and emotional well-being in all aspects of our work.
  • Transparency: Being open about how resources are used and sharing impact data with stakeholders.

What We Do

  • Weekly support meetings in 3 Matatiele communities
  • Career Pathway Workshop
  • Mental Health Awareness Sessions
  • Connections To Vocational Training
