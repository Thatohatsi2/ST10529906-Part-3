Weclome to THE NEW MAHLALELA

The New Mahlalela is a non-profit organisation based in Matatiele, Eastern Cape, dedicating to supporting post-matric youth aged 18 to 25. We provide mental health care, career pathway planning, and community connection to help young people build fulfilling lives

What We Offer

  • Mentall Health Support and Awareness Sessions
  • Career Pathways Workshops
  • TVET and Learnerships Connections
  • Weekly Community Support Meetings
